MEET BOB

Bob Erramilli is a distinguished leader with a unique combination of military service, business acumen, and community dedication. As a former combat pilot, he understands the importance of decisive leadership and strategic thinking under pressure.

In 2010, Bob established multiple medical practices focusing on providing exceptional healthcare services to the community. Through sound business principles and patient-centered care, he has built a reputation for excellence and reliability in the healthcare sector.

Bob has served his community and Johns Creek on numerous boards and organizations for over 15 years — including current City Council service, former HOA leadership, Sai Temple community programs, local charity initiatives, and various civic improvement projects.
