Our Commitment to Johns Creek

Bob Erramilli is focused on the critical issues that matter most to Johns Creek residents. His priorities are designed to ensure our city remains a safe, prosperous, and wonderful place to live, work, and raise a family.

01

Public Safety

Keeping our community safe and secure

Bob understands that public safety is the foundation of a thriving community. As a current City Council member, he has consistently supported initiatives that strengthen our police and fire departments while fostering positive community relationships.

  • Support for adequate police and fire department funding
  • Community policing initiatives to build trust and cooperation
  • Enhanced emergency response capabilities and equipment
  • Neighborhood watch programs and citizen engagement
  • Strategic planning for future safety infrastructure needs
02

Smart Growth & Development

Balanced development that preserves our quality of life

Johns Creek's growth must be managed thoughtfully to maintain the character and livability that makes our city special. Bob advocates for smart development that balances economic growth with community values and environmental stewardship.

  • Thoughtful zoning that protects residential neighborhoods
  • Strategic commercial development to support local economy
  • Preservation of green spaces and environmental resources
  • Infrastructure planning that accommodates sustainable growth
  • Community input in major development decisions
03

Fiscal Responsibility

Maximizing taxpayer value through efficient governance

As a successful business owner and healthcare professional, Bob brings proven financial management expertise to city governance. He is committed to ensuring every tax dollar is spent wisely and delivers maximum value to Johns Creek residents.

  • Transparent budget processes with community oversight
  • Efficient allocation of resources across city departments
  • Long-term financial planning for infrastructure needs
  • Regular audits and performance reviews of city programs
  • Competitive bidding processes for major city contracts
04

Traffic Management

Improving mobility and reducing congestion

Traffic congestion affects quality of life and economic vitality. Bob supports comprehensive transportation solutions that improve traffic flow while maintaining the safety and character of our neighborhoods.

  • Strategic traffic signal optimization and timing improvements
  • Road infrastructure improvements and maintenance programs
  • Pedestrian and bicycle safety enhancements
  • Coordination with regional transportation authorities
  • Smart technology solutions for traffic management
05

Parks & Recreation

Enhancing community spaces and recreational opportunities

Quality parks and recreation facilities are essential for community health, family enjoyment, and property values. Bob supports expanding and improving recreational opportunities that serve residents of all ages and interests.

  • Expansion and improvement of existing park facilities
  • Development of new recreational programs for all age groups
  • Maintenance and enhancement of walking trails and green spaces
  • Community sports facilities and youth athletic programs
  • Accessible recreation options for seniors and individuals with disabilities
06

Education Support

Partnering with schools for student success

While education is primarily managed at the county level, the city plays an important supporting role. Bob believes in fostering strong partnerships between the city and school system to ensure Johns Creek students have every opportunity to succeed.

  • Collaboration with Fulton County Schools on facility needs
  • Support for school safety and security initiatives
  • City programs that complement educational objectives
  • After-school and summer recreational programs for students
  • Community partnerships that enhance learning opportunities
